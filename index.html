OUR KICKBOXING WORKOUT

Kickboxing is an explosive, power endurance sport. Executing kicks and punches, round after round, hitting the heavy bag or boxing mitts challenges your power & endurance. Kickboxing is one of the most physically demanding sports of all.

DEVELOP SKILLS

The skill enhancement portion of your workout will consist of shadow boxing, heavy bag work, and focus mitts drills with a partner. Defense is an integral part of a boxer's skill set. Having the basic defensive techniques is essential to your development as a ighter. Learn how to slip and block kicks & punches to improve reaction time like a championship. Our fun, high-energy classes, provides a safe training environment for all students.

ROGER
DABNEY
Head Coach

Some of Dabney’s accomplishments include: 1981 Amateur Featherweight Kickboxing Champion; A Chosen Member of the 1984 United States 3-Man Karate Team; 1994 Virginia Welterweight Professional Kickboxing Champion; (PKC) Ranked in the Top Ten in the World in 1994. Dabney has also spent more than twenty years training in boxing gyms along the east coast as an amateur and professional boxer/kickboxer. He has trained with some of the top boxing coaches available and then returned back as a coach for many years.

STEVE “The Warrior”
MANN
Kickboxing Coach

Steve “The Warrior” Mann started his martial arts journey in 1992. He has trained with numerous celebrated martial artists, including Steve Burns, Clark “Thunder” Walters and Joe Lewis. His fighting debut was March 1st, 1997 and his titles include: Southwest Light Heavyweight, Championship WKKO 2008 and 14 other titles throughout his career. With a career Record of 34-6 with 10 TKO’s, Steve brings and unmatched enthusiasm, 10 years of training and coaching experience and his passion to all of CEK’s classes and training sessions.
